GONZO ACCUSED OF THE MURDER OF ROCKY

Written by Lauren Park

The murder that took the life of Rocky the Ram has sparked controversy as Gonzo is now accused of the murder. The previous suspect, Munson, was deemed too slow as he is a turtle. Gonzo, like Munson, has been trying to claim the spot of the Mascot of Rocky Run after failing to become President. If found guilty, he will be sentenced to life in prison for murder of the 1st degree. The court case starts on April 9th and experts say, "It is likely that Gonzo is not the murderer, but he is the closest suspect we got."
